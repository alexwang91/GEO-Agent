## Product Principles

- Measure real AI engine outputs when possible.
- Keep LLM simulation separate from real sampling.
- Diagnose why the brand is missing before generating content.
- Draft changes with evidence, impact, confidence, and risk.
- Do not auto-publish in the MVP.
- Preserve query, answer, citation, diagnosis, recommendation, and retest history.
